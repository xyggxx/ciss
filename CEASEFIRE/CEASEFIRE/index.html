DRY CHEMICAL
•  Puts out fire by coating the fuel with a thin layer of dust, separating the fuel from the oxygen in the air. The powder also works to interrupt the chemical reaction of fire, so these extinguishers are extremely effective at putting out fire.

SEE MORE

AFFF
•  Provides a fast, powerful means of tackling flammable liquids. The foam forms a seal over the surface to prevent re-ignition. Ideal for multi-risk usage. Most commonly used in offices, garage forecourts, factories and shops.

SEE MORE

CO2
•  Does not leave any residue, unlike a foam extinguisher. They can also be used on Class B fires, those involving flammable liquids such paraffin or petrol. CO2 extinguishers work by smothering the fire and cutting off the supply of air.

SEE MORE

HCFC
•  Has the same mode of action as powder extinguishers, as they interrupt the chemical chain reaction in the fire tetrahedron. They offer the additional advantage of being clean extinguishing agents, which leave no trace after the fire has been suppressed.

ABOUT US

Company Background

CEASEFIRE INDUSTRIAL SALES AND SERVICES, with office address at #16 Emilio Aguinaldo Highway, San Agustin 2, Dasmariñas, Cavite is exclusively a distribution, service company which renders and supplies Fire Fighting Appliances and Safety Equipments for more than a decade. Managed by Ms. VILMA V. ANTONIO and started from November 18, 1996 as a small distribution center in Makati City then preceded to Dasmariñas, Cavite as its market base up to present and has been successfully introduced and accepted by local businesses in the municipality. The complexities of business brought by technological advancement and challenges of the competition have boosted the owner and general manager of the company to pursue on its purpose not just to sell and service its prospective and valued clients but to help the same on whatever assistance the company could extend to continue their loyalty and patronage to Ceasefire. In search for wider market, the owner enforce on applying for quality certification mark which is accepted globally in order to compete with the increasing demands in business field. Fortunately, the company was granted and recognized to be one of the manufacturer in the province of Cavite carrying the following registered brands - FIRESHIELD, FIRE SLAYER, TITANIUM and BARRIER firefighting equipments under Bureau of Philippine (Product) Standards and presently aligned to PNS-ISO 9001:2015 for its Quality Management System.

Ceasefire Industrial and Safety Enterprise as manufacturer of Ceasefire Industrial Sales and Services, also owned and operated by Ms. Antonio, provides expertise in search quality marked Fire Fighting Equipment and Safety and Security Systems as solution to everyone’s fire safety needs and requirements. Now, guided with its principle to supply quality marked safety products, Ceasefire Industrial Sales and Services, hand in hand with Ceasefire Industrial and Safety Enterprises, caters to all types of occupancy such as Assembly, Educational, Health care, Detention & Correctional, Residential, Mercantile, Business, Industrial, Storage, Mixed Occupancies and Miscellaneous which cannot be properly classified in any of the preceding occupancy group.
